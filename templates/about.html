About

This is a simple web application built using Flask that allows you to search Wikipedia articles and bookmark your favorite pages.

The goal is to provide you with a user-friendly and efficient way to explore Wikipedia content and keep track of articles that interest you.

Feel free to use the search feature to find articles, save them for later reading, and learn more about the world's knowledge!
